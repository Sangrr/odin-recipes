Ceviche peuano de salmon

Salmon Peruvian ceviche is a popular dish in Peruvian cuisine that features fresh salmon marinated in a citrus-based sauce called leche de tigre. The salmon is typically cut into small cubes and mixed with sliced red onions, chili peppers, and cilantro, which give the dish its distinctive flavor and aroma.

The leche de tigre is made with lime juice, garlic, ginger, and other ingredients, and it is poured over the salmon just before serving, giving the dish a tangy and refreshing taste

Salmon Peruvian ceviche is often served as an appetizer or light lunch and is typically accompanied by sweet potatoes, corn on the cob, or avocado.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb fresh salmon, skinless and boneless, cut into small cubes
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1-2 hot chili peppers, such as jalape√±o or serrano, seeded and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated fresh ginger
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Sweet potatoes, boiled and sliced, for serving
  • Corn on the cob, boiled and sliced, for serving (optional
  • Avocado, sliced, for serving (optional)

Steps

  1. 1. Cut the salmon into small cubes and place them in a large mixing bowl.
  2. 2. Add the sliced red onions and hot chili peppers to the bowl with the salmon, and mix everything together.
  3. 3. In a separate mixing bowl, whisk together the lime juice, minced garlic, grated ginger, chopped cilantro, olive oil, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  4. 4. Pour the lime juice mixture over the salmon mixture and toss everything together until the salmon is fully coated in the marinade.
  5. 5. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld and the salmon to "cook" in the acidic marinade.
  6. 6. Remove the ceviche from the refrigerator and stir everything together again.
  7. 7. Serve the Salmon Peruvian ceviche in individual bowls or plates, along with boiled sweet potatoes, corn on the cob, or sliced avocado.
  8. 8. Garnish with additional cilantro, if desired.
  9. Enjoy your delicious and refreshing Salmon Peruvian ceviche!
