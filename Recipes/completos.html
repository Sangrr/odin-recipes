Traditional chilean completo

The "completo chileno" is a popular type of hot dog that originated in Chile and has become an important part of the country's culinary culture. It consists of a soft, fluffy hot dog bun that is filled with a Vienna sausage, accompanied by a variety of toppings such as chopped tomatoes, sliced avocado (known as "palta" in Chile), mayonnaise, mustard, ketchup, sauerkraut, and American sauce. The combination of toppings varies according to each person's preference, but generally the completo chileno is very generous and loaded with many condiments.

The completo chileno is considered a very popular fast food and can be found in a wide variety of places, from street food stands to fast food restaurants and convenience stores. It is a very popular food at sporting events, parties, and fairs, and is an integral part of Chilean culinary culture.

Ingredients

  1. A hot dog bun
  2. A Vienna sausage or hot dog
  3. Chopped tomatoes
  4. Sliced avocado
  5. Mayonnaise
  6. Mustard
  7. Ketchup
  8. Sauerkraut
  9. American sauce

Steps

  1. 1. Preheat your grill or a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. 2. Place the Vienna sausages or hot dogs on the grill or skillet and cook them until they are browned on all sides.
  3. 3. While the sausages are cooking, slice the tomatoes and avocado.
  4. 4. Warm the hot dog buns by placing them on the grill or skillet for a few seconds until they are slightly toasted.
  5. 5. Once the sausages are cooked, remove them from the grill or skillet and place them inside the toasted hot dog buns.
  6. 6. Add the chopped tomatoes and sliced avocado on top of the sausages.
  7. 7. Add a generous amount of mayonnaise, mustard, ketchup, and sauerkraut on top of the tomatoes and avocado
  8. 8. If desired, add a drizzle of American sauce on top of the other condiments.
  9. 9. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious completo!
